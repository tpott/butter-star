DUST BUSTERS

A Butter* Production

The game is simple: suck up as many dustbunnies as you can before time runs out! The player who sucks up the most bunnies wins bragging rights for all eternity!

Use A, W, S, and D to move your character left, forwards, backwards, and right (respectively) and the spacebar to suck up those gosh-darned rabbits. You can also use the mouse to control your view. You can see other players' scores by pressing TAB and if you ever need to see the menu, just press ESC!
